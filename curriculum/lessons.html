Intro to Makey Makey

Makey Makey is a microcontroller which is preprogrammed so a student can pulg and play, think of Arduino with training wheels. It teaches students about the flow of eletrical currents, enginneering, prototyping and problem solving. Students will learn how to make a piano out of bananas, and a game controller. In the final lesson of the introduction the students will create and film their own working Makey Makey project.
